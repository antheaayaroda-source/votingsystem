Election Rules and Guidelines

✨TEAM GALING✨

"Excellence in Action, Leadership with Integrity"

EXCELLENCE FIRST
  • Every member must give their best in both academics and leadership to inspire others.
INTEGRITY AND HONESTY
  • Never spread rumors or fake information about other candidates.
  • Always be truthful in all campaign materials.
RESPECT AND FAIRNESS
  • Respect everyone's opinion, even those who support the other team.
  • Treat all students equally — no favoritism.
LEAD BY EXAMPLE
  • Be punctual, responsible, and kind so that others can see your leadership through your actions.

VISION FOR ALL

  • Focus your campaign on how your plans will benefit the entire student body, not just your friends or class.

✨TEAM SIGLA✨

"Energy, Unity, and Action!"

BE A ROLE MODEL
  • Every member must show positivity, discipline, and teamwork at all times.
CAMPAIGN WITH ENERGY AND RESPECT
  • Always promote the team's goals in a cheerful and respectful way.
  • No negative words or insults toward other candidates.
PROMOTE SCHOOL SPIRIT
  • Encourage classmates to join school activities and promote unity through small acts of kindness.
CREATIVE AND HONEST CAMPAIGN
  • Use creative posters, videos, and slogans that reflect the team's true mission.
  • Be honest about what you can do - never make false promises.
SERVE BEFORE YOU LEAD
  • Show leadership through service. Help others first, even before asking for votes.
